The Chamber of Reflection

The chamber was cold with nearly the whole room covered by a pool of water. In the centre stood seven white pillars with a black platform raised around them. Heat came from flower like lamps at the centre.

In the waters, you see yourself. Every decision you made in the maze plays out in silvery images. The paths you chose. The dangers you faced.

At the far end it leads to another chamber.
